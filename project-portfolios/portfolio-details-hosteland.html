HostelAnd - A User-Centric Hostel Booking App for University Students

HostelAnd, is a testament to the power of user-centered design. HostelAnd is a dedicated hostel booking app crafted specifically for university students in Kampala, Uganda. The goal was to transform the often tedious and stressful process of finding suitable accommodation into a seamless, intuitive, and engaging experience. By combining in-depth user research, thoughtful information architecture, and interactive design, I created an app that not only meets the practical needs of students but also delights them with its usability and aesthetics. Dive into this case study to explore the complete journey from concept to high-fidelity designs and discover how each design decision was driven by real user insights.
